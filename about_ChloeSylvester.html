Just imagine you are on a plane and it crashes and you are stranded in the middle of the ocean and fish, sharks, and whatever else is in the water is stalking you because they are curious to see what you are. Or if you are stuck out there for weeks suffering when I would have rather just passed away on impact than suffer.The open ocean is my biggest fear, but I also do not like spiders, snakes, sometimes heights and the thought of potentially being kidnapped.
